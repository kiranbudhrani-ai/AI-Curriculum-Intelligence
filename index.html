AI Across the Curriculum Growth Signals

Evidence-based insights on AI visibility, curriculum growth, and emerging opportunities across the curriculum.

This resource hub helps academic leaders understand how artificial intelligence is appearing across the undergraduate and graduate curriculum. The reports use catalog-level signals from course titles, descriptions, and effective dates to show where AI is visible, where it is embedded, and where curriculum growth is accelerating.

How to Navigate: Start with the Executive Summary for the leadership story, then use the Full Dashboard, Explorer, and College Scorecards for deeper review.

What This Measures

This site measures catalog-level AI signals. It does not measure enrollment, section offerings, student demand, faculty readiness, course quality, or depth of AI use in instruction.
