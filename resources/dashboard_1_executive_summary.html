What leadership should take from this

Three sentences, no scrolling required.

AI has moved from concentrated to catalog-wide. Computing & Informatics still leads with the highest penetration (13.3% of its scanned courses), but AI signals now span 26 disciplines/course types across 6 colleges.

2026 is the inflection point. Of the 89 AI-signal records with a usable effective date, 46 — more than half — became effective in 2026 alone, versus 43 across every prior year combined back to 2017.

Title visibility understates the footprint. 50 of 117 records (Tier 2) mention AI only in the course description, not the title — meaning a title-only scan of the catalog would miss nearly half of where AI activity already lives.

This measures catalog-level AI signals and timing, not enrollment, section capacity, or instructional depth. Full detail available in the companion dashboard and course inventory.
